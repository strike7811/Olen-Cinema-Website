Frequently Asked Questions

General Information


I left my item in your cinema hall. How can I retrieve it?

You can proceed to the cinema location or contact our Customer Relations at (603) 7888 7988 (Monday - Friday from 9am to 6pm excluding Public Holidays) or email to OlenCinemas@movie.com for assistance.

I'm interested to join Olen Cinemas. Is there any vacancy?

Please email your resume to hr@OlenCinemas.com . Our HR team will contact you directly if you are shortlisted.

What are the restrictions applied to patrons?

Customers must adhere to the movie classification provided by LPF Malaysia as per below:-

U - General viewing for all ages
P13 - Parental guidance is advisable for children below 13 years old
18 - For 18+ with elements for mature audiences (violence, horror, sex, politics, religion, counter-culture, etc.)

Movies In Olen Cinemas


I have an infant, 4 months old. Can I bring him / her together with me for a movie?

Yes, but only for movies classified as 'U' or 'P13'.

My children are aged below 18 years old. Can I bring them for a movie classified '18'?

Kindly be reminded that this film is for patrons above the age of 18 only. We may verify your age against your NRIC.

My children are aged below 13 years old. Can I bring them for a movie classified 'P13'?

Yes, children below 13 years old are allowed with parental guidance.

How long will a movie usually be screened at Olen Cinemas?

A movie will normally be screened for at least 2 weeks, or longer, depending on market demand.

Can I bring in outside food into Olen Cinemas?

Food purchase outside of Olen Cinemas permises will not be allowed in the cinema / movie halls. If you had purchased any outside food, you may place them at the ticket tearing point before entering the cinema / movie halls.

New Sign-Up

How do I sign up to purchase e-ticket?

Click on the Log In button located at the top-right corner of the page. Make sure you have logged into your Olen Cinemas Account. Once your account is verified and registered, you will be able to purchase e-ticket from Olen Cinemas web.

I forgot the password used for the Olen Cinemas account. What can I do?

1. Click on the Log In button.
2. Click on FORGOT PASSWORD?.
3. Click OK.
4. Please check your email to obtain a link that redirects you to reset your password.
5. You may log in with your new password now.

Sales & Service Tax Implementation

With the implementation of SST from 1st September 2018, how will it affect Olen Cinemas customers? (i.e. Will your ticket prices go up?)

The 6% Service Tax applies to movie tickets and all products sold as part of our F&B. This includes takeaways, concession food items, and combos sold at our cafes, concessions counters, and online.
Prices will be rounded to the nearest 10 sen. (e.g. RM9.53 becomes RM9.50 / RM9.57 becomes RM9.60).

  Current F&B Price   F&B with 6% Service Tax
Net Product Price 8.00 8.00
6% Service Tax - 0.48
Sub-Total 8.00 8.48
Total (after rounding) 8.00 8.50

A Guide to the Olen Chat Bot

How can I interact with the Olen Chat Bot?

The chat bot is located at the bottom right corner of our website with an icon of our company logo. Once it is clicked, a chat box will appear. You may ask about the showtime of a movie, location of our cinemas, and even prices of our food and beverage there. For further assistance, please direct to our Chat Bot Guide Page.
