Rest in Peace Department

Genre: Action, Comedy, Supermatural

Release Date: 19 July 2023











Director:
Robert Schwentke
Distributor:
Universal Pictures
Run Time: 1h 36m
R.I.P.D., also marketed as R.I.P.D.: Rest in Peace Department, is an American supernatural action comedy film starring Jeff Bridges and Ryan Reynolds. The film was directed by Robert Schwentke and written by Phil Hay and Matt Manfredi, based on the comic book R.I.P.D. by Peter M. Lenkov.

A cop killed by his own partner joins RIPD, an afterlife law enforcement department working to apprehend various monsters disguised as humans living on earth, and gets paired up with a smart-mouthed veteran.
