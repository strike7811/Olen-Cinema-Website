About Us


We have delighted numerous moviegoers with memories of a fantastic day out since officially opening our doors in 2003. With Olen Cinema's broad selection of entertainment, there is something for everyone, from the newest blockbusters to intimate dramas, with a splash of documentaries, sports, and culture all in the mix.


Relax in the luxurious comfort of our relaxing venues or indulge in the luxuries of Malaysia's top luxury cinema. Bring the family along for some family-friendly entertainment, or treat your eyes to the biggest cinema LED screen in the world, with its astounding clarity. Finally, immerse yourself in the spectacular film experience, which is the most realistic on the planet. You get to decide!


We also take pleasure in the flavorful quality of our food and beverages, providing customers at any of our 8 locations around the country with the greatest popcorn in town as well as an intriguing array of options. From the innocent beauty of a first date to the first time that children see their superheroes come to life on the big screen, we consider ourselves fortunate and incredibly gratified to have had a little role in significant occasions.
